Simple omelette recipe

Dive into the art of making the perfect omelette,a versatile and delicious dish that can be customised to suit any taste.

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt for taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: Cheese, vegetables

Instructions

1. Crack 2-3 eggs into a bowl.
2. Add a splash of milk or cream to the eggs (optional).
3. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
4. Whisk the eggs until well combined and slightly frothy.
5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil.
6. Once the pan is hot, pour in the egg mixture.
7. Let the eggs sit for a few seconds until they start to set around the edges.
8. Using a spatula, gently push the cooked edges towards the center while tilting the pan to let the uncooked egg flow underneath.
9. Continue this process until most of the liquid is gone but there are still some creamy curds remaining. 10. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
Enjoy your delicious scrambled eggs!
